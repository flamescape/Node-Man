What's this?

Node-Man is a re-imagining of Namco's classic arcade game with added multiplayer game mechanics.

You get to either play as the game's titular hero with the goal of gobbling up all the nodes distributed around the map, or play as one of the antagonists represented as the ghosts of dead web technologies.

Spectators are queued to play until a slot opens!
